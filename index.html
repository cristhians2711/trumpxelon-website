About the Project

Meowlands is an NFT art project blending abstract dreamscapes and enigmatic cats into 1,000 unique tokens minted on Solana. Each piece invites holders into a world of introspection, visual poetry, and soft rebellion — while contributing 20% of profits to verified animal shelters.
